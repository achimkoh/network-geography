history
dates:
1845: First commercial telegraph system
1851: First submarine telegraph cable laid between Dover and Calais
1856: Cyrus Field forms the Atlantic Telegraph Company
1866: Atlantic Telegraph Company, with Telcon, successfully complete transatlantic telegraph cable
1880: First development of coaxial cable
1930: First telephone submarine cable laid
1956: TAT-1, the first transatlantic telephone cable goes into service
1986: First optical submarine cable laid between Honshu and Hokkaido
1988: TAT-8, the first transatlantic optical cable, goes into service
ownership: consortiums of telecom companies (e.g. AT&T, BT, Level 3, China Telecom, Tata Communications)
construction costs: very expensive
